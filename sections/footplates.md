# Footplates

We have several different steering systems across boats.

## Steering wire

They can generally all use the same wire: 2 mm high-grade [PVC-coated stainless rope (7x7)][wire-rope]. Ideally we buy this direct (~£0.44/m) rather than paying about £3/m from a rowing shop.

[wire-rope]: https://www.wireropeshop.co.uk/clear-coated-pvc-stainless-steel-wire-rope-7x7/
