## Maintenance

Day to day processes to look after the boats, incuding being proactive about preventing damage.

- Pre-outing checks
  - heel restraints
  - seat
  - bowball
  - bolts
  - footplate
- Post-outing clean
  - soap
  - water
  - towel
  - hull
  - inside
  - runners
  - open hatch covers
- Deep clean
  - Hull cleaner
  - Vinegar
  - Remove all parts
  - Check for missing parts
  ...
- Polish
- Rigger crack check
- Damage triage
- Rudder alignment
- Verify hardware (washers etc)
- Replace oar handle/suede
- Replace hatch cover cord
- Report/tag broken boats
- Gate bushings
- Trestles
